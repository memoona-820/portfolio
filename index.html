A little about me

I'm a web developer who believes great digital products are built at the intersection of clean code and human-centered thinking. I don't just build what looks good — I build what works well for the people using it.

On the UI side, I craft pixel-perfect, responsive layouts using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — making sure every button, spacing, and color choice serves a purpose.

On the UX side, I think deeply about user journeys: how someone moves through a page, where they get confused, what makes them trust a product, and how small design decisions reduce friction and increase satisfaction.

I believe accessible, fast, and intuitive interfaces aren't a bonus — they're the baseline.
